RSS Feed

What is RSS?

RSS is a data format that allows you to receive regular updates from EventBee without logging in to your EventBee account. At present EventBee generates RSS feed for Discussion Forums, if you subscribe to this RSS feed, you can receive a notice of each new posting in the Forum - as soon as it's posted.

How do I receive an RSS feed?

To add Forum RSS feed to your reader, visit Hub page, copy and paste RSS Feed URL displayed next to the RSS image in the Forum to your reader

Where can I download an RSS reader?
EventBee's RSS feeds are built to Web standards, so they should work with any RSS readers/aggregator. We've tested our feeds with the following program:

SharpReader: http://www.sharpreader.com (Free windows RSS reader)

To find other RSS readers, please enter "news aggregator" or "RSS reader" into your preferred search engine.

How do I respond to a message I receive via RSS?

To post a response in a discussion thread that you're following via RSS, you'll need to log in to your EventBee account and return to the Forum in question. Find the posting to which you wish to respond, and click "Post Reply" button.

Can I add an RSS feed to my blog?
Sure! Adding EventBee RSS feeds to your blog lets you reach a wider audience, bringing your readers into the EventBee where you participate :)

Can I add an RSS feed to My Yahoo?

Yes, we are participating in Add to My Yahoo beta program, to add Forum RSS feed to your yahoo, just click on "+ My Y!" button displayed in the Forum.
