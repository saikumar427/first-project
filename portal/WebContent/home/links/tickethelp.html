Ticketing TIPS

Required Tickets

Attendee is required to select one of these tickets to continue registration process.

Optional Tickets

Attendee can choose multiple types of these tickets after selecting one Required Ticket.

Ticket Group

To display similar tickets together, create Ticket Group, add tickets under the Ticket Group.

Ticket Display Ordering

Click on Up/Down arrow buttons to change tickets display order viewed by Attendee.

Early Bird Discount

You can offer Early Bird Discount by creating two tickets of same type and setting start, end times of the tickets accordingly.

Group Discount

Offer Group Discount by setting minimum number of tickets required to purchase to greater than 1.

Discount Codes

You can create discount codes for any ticket type from Event Manage page. List your event, then visit Event Manage page to create Discount Codes for various tickets.
