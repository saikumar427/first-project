Use Cases
 
Meeting planners
Basic - If you just need to power your event with simple online registration features, this is the right account for you.
Gold - Run email campaigns. Automatically create Attendee mailing lists for future reference. Design unique campaigns with pre-populated event information including sponsorship details and Online registration links.
Platinum - This account allows you to create multiple roles and users with controlled access to various event related tasks. Use this account if there are multiple users responsible for producing your event. Create surveys and polls to use across multiple events.
Activity professionals
Community Basic - If you are managing activities which involve group of people, (training sessions, sports events, travel and tours, etc.), this account is perfect for you. Engage your members by providing easy to use tools and communication mechanisms to help foster a community feeling. Powering your website with Unit Basic allows you to create personalized home pages for each user and encourage social networking. You can ask pre-event survey questions to customize your user's experience or conduct post activity surveys to measure your success and plan future improvements. By giving your users permanent access to activity resources, you can maintain long-term relationships with each user and build customer loyalty.
Corporations
Community Basic - This account helps you shorten your lead time and facilitates your email marketing, attendee registration, database management, creative and logistic processes as you plan company-wide training programs, product launches, employee incentive, sales conferences or customer satisfaction events.
Zones
Community Gold - This account contains all the great event management features of a Community Basic level and also allows you to create different membership types. Users can choose membership type and setup recurring membership fee. Each member gets a customized home page. Create discussion forums, classifieds, accessible only to your members. Maximize all Community Gold account features and leverage integrated event tools to effectively manage association events. You can custom design and e-blast your association newsletters using email campaign feature. Effectively manage members, control and authenticate member status.
Non-profits
Basic, Gold, Platinum - Eventbee provides these account free of charge to qualified Non-profits. Choose your account based on your requirements and manage your fund raising, community, charity events and member activities cost effectively.
