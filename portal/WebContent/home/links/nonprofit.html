Non-profit accounts
Eventbee is fully committed to community by offering Basic, Basic PLUS, Group, Community accounts free-of-charge to Qualified Nonprofit Organizations.

If you signup as a Non-profit organization, your account approval is subject to submission and review of the following documents.


1.Copy of 501(c)(3)
2.One page summary of your organization

Please email support@eventbee.com and fax the above documents to 408 421 8149 after signing up as a Non-profit Organization account. Make sure to check the Nonprofit Organization checkbox during the signup process, otherwise, your account will be registered as a regular account and you will be required to provide payment information and submit appropriate payments.
