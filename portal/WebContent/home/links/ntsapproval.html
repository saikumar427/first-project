Network Ticket Selling Approval

Auto

This option approves all existing Partners on Eventbee system to sell your event tickets and get commission on each ticket sale. New Partners who want to participate in ticket selling process are auto approved to sell your event tickets.

Manual

If this option is set to Manual, you can pick & choose Partners to sell your event tickets. If any Partner shows interest in participating ticket selling process, you will receive a notification and you can review the Partner before approval.
