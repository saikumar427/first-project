Payment Processing Methods & Pricing

What is the fee?

Service Fee: $1 per ticket, regardless of ticket price and currency!

Credit Card Processing Fee: You will pay this fee to CC Processing provider. At present we support PayPal, Google and Eventbee CC Processing.

You can find PayPal processing fee by visiting http://www.paypal.com/us/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_display-fees-outside

You can find Google processing fee by visiting https://checkout.google.com/sell

If you chose Eventbee for CC Processing, we charge 4.95% + 50c per transaction. We do accounts first week of the month and mail check for the events closed in prior month. Also, our credit card processing company holds 10% of the proceeds for 60 days to take care of any chargebacks.

How do I integrate with my Google Account?

1. Login to your Google checkout account

2. Go to Settings tab, click on Integration link in left side menu

3. You will see your Merchant ID and Key, copy/paste these values in your Google Payment Method selection

4. Uncheck "My company will only post digital signed carts"

5. Enter following URL as API call back URL:

https://www.eventbee.com/portal/payment/googlecallback

Make sure API Callback Method set to XML and SAVE

6. Go to Preferences link in the left side menu, and select following option and SAVE

Automatically authorize and charge the buyer's credit card.
