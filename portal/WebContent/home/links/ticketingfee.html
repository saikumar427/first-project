Ticketing Fee

Eventbee Service Fee

Flat $1 fee per ticket, you can pass this fee to attendee by setting Ticketing Fee to $1

Credit Card Processing Fee

You pay this fee to credit card processing provider. You can pass this fee to attendee by adding it to Ticketing Fee

If you chose PayPal or Google as your credit card processing provider, they charge anywhere from 2% to 2.9% + some fixed fee per transaction.

If you chose Eventbee for credit card processing, we charge 4.95% + 50c per transaction.

Do you recommend passing Ticketing Fee to Attendee?

Most of the event managers include Ticketing Fee in the Ticket Price itself. Having said that, it is completely your decision to collect Ticketing Fee from Attendee. We don't restrict how much you collect as Ticketing Fee from Attendee, if you would like you can even add administrative costs to the Ticketing Fee.
