Frequently asked questions
 

What is Eventbee?

Eventbee is a Indian community portal where members can -

- Create local special interest groups

- Find and post classifieds

- Create networking-oriented home page

- Build business and personal relationships

- Manage public and private events

- Keep track of friends and family

- Make new friends through existing friends

- Volunteer for the community cause

Is this service Free?

Yes, all of the Eventbee community features are FREE.

How does Eventbee generate revenues?

Eventbee revenue model is based on online advertisements.

How does Eventbee uses its revenues?

Eventbee has the goal of contributing portion of its advertisement revenues towards Drinking water problem in India. Remaining revenues are used to run operations.

Is Eventbee Non-profit organization?

Eventbee is not a Nonprofit organization. We believe that we got a unique opportunity to build a profitable business while contributing towards drinking water problem in India

"India had built huge steel plants, launched rockets and satellites and produced missiles. But at the same time 300 million people are illiterate, 50 per cent of the classes have one teacher, 250 million people do not have safe drinking water and 750 million do not have access to proper sanitation'' - Narayana Murthy, Infosys Chairman

How does Eventbee distribute money towards Drinking water problem?

Eventbee works with number of established Indian Non-profits and achieves its goal by funding Drinking water projects. If you are a Nonprofit, and interested working with Eventbee, please send email to nonprofits@eventbee.com

How do I get started using Eventbee?

Signup as a member by clicking Sign Up link on the Eventbee home page. Login to your account, update your profile and build your public home page.

I already have account but forgot my account details.

Click on Login help link, enter required information to get your Desi ID or Password. If you do not have all the required information to get your account details automatically, please send email to support@eventbee.com with your complete profile information.

I am getting "Session expired" error message.

For security reasons we expire active sessions after certain amount of time. If you receive this error consistently, please launch new browser window to access Eventbee site.

How do I report abuse?

Eventbee is dedicated to making its community experience as professional as possible. If you see anyone abusing Eventbee site, please send email to abuse@eventbee.com. We thank you for taking the initiative in reporting abuse to us!
