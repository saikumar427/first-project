Step 1 - Visit Beeport
Learn Beeport Manager account options. Beeport offers FREE Basic account which allows FREE listing of events at eventbee.com.
Step 2 - Signup Manager Account
Signup for the Manager account which suites your requirements. If you are opening Community and above check List at eventbee.com option to list your Association or Club or Group.
 
 
Step 3 - Login as Manager at Beeport
Step 4A - Create Events
Check List at eventbee.com option during event creation.
 
Step 4B - Create Sponsorships
Check List at eventbee.com option during sponsorships creation. If your manager account is Community and above you can create sponsorships for Community (Association or Club or Group).
